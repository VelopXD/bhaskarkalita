About Bhaskar Kalita

Dedicated public servant committed to the development and welfare of the people

Political Journey

Bhaskar Kalita has been a dedicated member of the Bharatiya Janata Party for over 15 years, serving the people of Assam with unwavering commitment. His political journey began as a grassroots worker, where he gained firsthand experience of the challenges faced by common citizens.
